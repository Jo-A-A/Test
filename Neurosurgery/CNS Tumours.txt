CNS Tumours
What is the most common primary brain tumor in adults?
A) Meningioma
B) Pituitary adenoma
C) Glioblastoma multiforme (GBM)
D) Ependymoma
Correct Answer: C) Glioblastoma multiforme (GBM)
###
Which of the following tumors has the WORST prognosis (mean survival)?
A) Anaplastic astrocytoma
B) Untreated prolactinoma
C) Hemangioblastoma with von Hippel-Lindau
D) Glioblastoma multiforme (GBM)
Correct Answer: D) Glioblastoma multiforme (GBM)
###
Which of the following statements regarding Medulloblastoma is WRONG?
A) It is the most common malignant primary brain tumor in children
B) It is a small cell embryonal tumor that typically arises in the cerebellar vermis
C) It is classified as WHO Grade I because it occurs in children
D) It often presents with signs and symptoms of hydrocephalus
Correct Answer: C) It is classified as WHO Grade I because it occurs in children
